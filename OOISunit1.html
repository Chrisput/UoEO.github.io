Unit 1: Introduction to Information Systems

On completion of this unit I am able to

  • Understand the core elements of an information system

  • Gain an understanding of the SDLC, its phases and their importance

Reflections

Below are my key learnings and reflections

  • The case studay on Air Traffic system failure provided a critical analysis on the issues around this incident, the impacts on the organisation and wider community, as well as how this could have been avoided
  • Research on further Information System failures, helped me identify one of the larges ERP implementation failures which eventually led to the bankruptcy of the company
  • Cybersecurity protects computers, networks, and data from cybercrime. The most common forms of attack are malware and Denial of Service, common protection is through firewall, proxy server, and auditing and antivirus software. Cryptography is used to send and receive messages securily.
