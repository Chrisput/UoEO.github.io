Launching into Computer Science

This module will explore and investigate the fundamental theories and practices of computing and than gradually explore the trends and current developments in the field of computer science.

Learning outcomes

I will have learned:

  • to identify and explain the architecture, structure and functionality of basic components of a computer system.
  • to demonstrate a critical uderstanding of core data structures and programming concepts including algorythm computability.
  • to critically evaluate the functionality of different types of software, i.e. operating systems, utility programmes, languages and applications.
  • to critically appraise the emerging trends in the field, such as cloud computing, big data, cyber security, and the professionaland ethical requirements for dealing with such contemporary computer-based technologies.

Unit 1: Computing as a Profession - Responsibilities and Challenges

On completion of this unit I will be able to: appreciate roles and responsibilities of computer professionals, examine the various challenges and threats a computer professional may deal with, and apply the professional ethics and code of conduct in computing practices
