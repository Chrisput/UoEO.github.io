Launching into Computer Science - Complete

This module explores and investigates the fundamental theories and practices of computing and than gradually explores the trends and current developments in the field of computer science.

Learning outcomes

This module has enabled me:

  • to identify and explain the architecture, structure and functionality of basic components of a computer system.
  • to demonstrate a critical uderstanding of core data structures and programming concepts including algorythm computability.
  • to critically evaluate the functionality of different types of software, i.e. operating systems, utility programmes, languages and applications.
  • to critically appraise the emerging trends in the field, such as cloud computing, big data, cyber security, and the professionaland ethical requirements for dealing with such contemporary computer-based technologies.

Tutor: Dr Nawaz Khan

Core reading book for this module: Computer Science - An Overview by J. Glenn Brookshear and Dennis Brylow
