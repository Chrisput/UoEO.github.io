Unit 12: Research Topics in Computer Science - Data Science, AI and Standards

On completion of this unit I am able to

  • Explore the trends and research topics in the area of computer science.
  • Identify and define a problem in the area of computer science.
  • Propose a research question and then support the research question with adequate literature-based evidence.

Reflections

Below are my key learnings and reflections

  • Exploring models and methods used in machine learning and apply them to real-world situations
  • Exploring concepts of supervised as well as unsupervised learning techniques. In supervised we develop a predictive model based on on both input and output data. In unsupervised we group and interpret data based only on input data.
  • Machine Learning is used for trend analysis, predicting behaviour, and time-series analysis.
  • Artificial Intelligence can be used for images (pattern matching, image analysis, object recognition) and for language (topic search, semantics analysis, natural language processing, context aware reasoning).
  • Internet of Things referes to network of connected machines and hand-held smart devices so they can interact with each other, sending and receiving instructions.
